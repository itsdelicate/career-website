Becoming a Photographer

Photography is more than just taking pictures, it's an art form that captures emotions, tells stories, and preserves moments in time. I have always been fascinated by how a single image can express what words sometimes cannot. My dream is to become a professional photographer who can communicate powerful narratives through visuals. I am particularly inspired by Natt Lim, also known as Nattografi, whose documentary and portrait photography resonates deeply with me. Through photography, I hope to explore the world, connect with people, and create meaningful content that inspires others.
