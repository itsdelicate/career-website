Natt Lim (Nattografi)

Natt Lim, widely known by his online persona Nattografi, is a Malaysian photographer whose work centers around portrait and documentary photography. His unique ability to capture raw human emotions and genuine moments has made him one of the most recognizable creatives in the local photography scene.

Natt's style is natural, heartfelt, and often cinematic his photos don't just show people, they tell their stories. Whether it's a quiet street scene, a behind-the-scenes moment with a celebrity, or a deeply personal portrait, his images have a strong emotional impact. What sets him apart is his consistency in keeping his work authentic and meaningful, avoiding over editing or staged perfection.

He is also known for collaborating with brands and publications, but remains grounded in his storytelling roots. Through Instagram @nattografi, he shares his work and creative process, often inspiring aspiring photographers like me to stay true to our voice and passion.
